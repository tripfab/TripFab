Get Paid

After we told you about thousands more reservations, wondering how you get paid?

We Give You Lots Of Options

After someone books a reservation with you, we go ahead and process that payment and as soon as your guests arrive we go ahead and release that payment over to your account. You can either choose to withdraw that right away, or maybe you have lots of reservations coming in that same week, and you prefer to wait a few days and batch together the payments into one large withdrawal. Whatever you want.

TripFab Debit Card

Every business gets issues an official TripFab card, which gives you access to your money almost instantly. You can go to the ATM, purchase things, or whatever else you like. It’s absolutely free.

Bank Transfer

We can send you a wire transfer from our bank over to the valid bank account you have on file. Usually this takes a few days depending on your bank.

PayPal

If you have a PayPal account, we can go ahead and debit over your paypal account with the funds available in your account.

TripFab Fees
We charge a 5% per transaction fee, plus the 2.5% credit card processing fee that is passed along to you.

No monthly fee, no contract - nothing. We get paid when you get paid. That simple.
