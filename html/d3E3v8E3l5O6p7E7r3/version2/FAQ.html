About Tripfab

Who makes Tripfab?

Tripfab is a revolutionary new way to travel.

We are not another travel agency in disguise, looking for fat commission cuts from hotels and tour operators – which you, the consumer, end up paying for. Our cutting-edge travel marketplace gives you the power to be your own travel agent. You can easily contact any business on our site directly, at no cost to you. Read our detailed hotel and activity descriptions, (or contact companies directly via email, live chat or VoiP phone call), gathering all the information you need to plan your trip when you want it – any way you want it.

With no middlemen

As a marketplace, we are putting the power back into your hands, as well as those of our partners – meaning you get better services at a better price. So no more 25% to 40% commissions for travel agents and impersonal websites that are only interested in making quick sales and offer nightmarish service and support. Tripfab is here to change all that.

We believe the travel industry should be better, and we’ve set out to change it. Tripfab is much more than just the tools we offer – it’s a whole new way of looking at travel. We know things won’t change in a day. But together with our partners, and of course you (our friends and clients), we will make the travel industry everything it should be and more: a fabulous and affordable experience every time, all the time.

The Tripfab Vision: Making your Trip Fabulous

We have a vision. When you take a trip, it shouldn’t be about simply getting to your destination, looking around and going back home. It should be an enlightening, and even life-changing experience. Traveling should not just be going from one place to another, but a fabulous process from start to finish.

And to accomplish this we have not only built tools to help you plan and execute your vacation or business trip, but we will continue building and expanding them. Best of all, we back it all up with talent – the best of the best in the travel industry. Our fabulous team of real, live people (not automated robots or barely bilingual zombies in some developing country) are here to help and advise you every step of the way. If you need help booking your trip, organizing your itinerary or answering any questions regarding the country you are visiting we are here for you. But the buck doesn’t stop there. Tripfab travels with you, wherever you go. We are available at all of our destinations 24/7 to help you with any issues or questions you may have throughout your trip.

Making your trip fabulous is not simply a slogan: it’s what we are out to set as the industry standard. Traveling should always be personalized. It’s about you, the traveler, and your experience – not about numbers of bookings, and squeezing every possible penny out of your pocket.
