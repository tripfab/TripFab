Highlights

Monteverde Cloud Forest

Nothing can quite prepare you for your first visit to the cloud forest of Monteverde. Walking through the upper reaches of the canopy is like entering another world, one that is more mystery than reality and more ghost than substance. Each step you take into the wispy air of the cloud forest brings you closer to the amazing biodiversity of the region and is a lesson unto itself in how jungles are nourished by these mysterious clouds of moisture.

Overview

12 DAYS

Days / 11 Nights Trip

Let the great outdoors itself be your classroom on this educational journey across Costa Rica. No ecology lesson can compare with the tutorial you’ll receive as you walk the flanks of an ancient volcano and study the steam that still percolates from the crater – or as you journey deep into the cloud forest and observe the incredible amount of biodiversity present in the unique environs. While the opportunities for education are immense on this trip, there is also plenty of time for adventure and exploration, along with a chance for some rest and relaxation on one of Costa Rica’s most beautiful beaches.
