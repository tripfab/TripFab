Package Overview

9 Days / 8 Nights Trip

Explore the best of what Costa Rica has to offer at a leisurely pace. Designed for older tourists hoping to see the jewels of Central America on a tour that caters to a cultured crowd, this trip will incorporate five-star accommodations and dining with outdoor adventure, educational and cultural activities and opportunities for quiet afternoons and evenings.

Step away from the surfers and the backpackers and enjoy some of the more quiet settings in this beautiful country. In addition to some of the best natural sights in country, you will visit coffee plantations and traditional Central American ranches on a VIP tour that pairs exploration with elegance.

More details about the package
Fast Facts
  • • It takes between 32 and 36 hours to reach Cocos Island
  • • There are roughly 20 dives sites located around the island
  • • Cocos was recognized as a World Heritage Site in 1997
  • • Bajo Alcyone is the number one diving spot to see hammerhead sharks in action
  • • Cocos Island is situated in the Golden Triangle, off the coast of mainland Costa Rica
  • • Most dives on the trip will take place between 60 and 130 feet below the surface of the water
  • • Average water temperature is 79 to 84 degrees Fahrenheit
