The position

We are looking for a freelance travel writer, to come with us and tour around cities around the world and interview a wide variety of travel businesses and write an in-depth description about them.

All expenses paid. Yea - an awesome job, we know.

Your first trip will be Costa Rica, and we will be going on a 3-5 week tour all around the country. On the first Trip, you will be with our CEO as well as teamed up with a photographer.

Some things you need to write per business:

  • About the location
  • What you like
  • What travelers should know and expect
  • Why they should go there
  • An honest review
  • How to get there
  • Things to do

So who are we looking for?

  • We are looking for an extremely strong writer, predominately in english
  • You need to have above average writing and interviewing skills
  • You need to speak at the very least, excellent Spanish and English
  • Someone responsible, independent and a team player
  • Extremely detail oriented
  • Friendly, approachable and professional
  • You should love traveling
